Non AC Rooms

NRs. 1200/Night

A non-AC room in a hotel can be a great option for those looking to save money on their stay without sacrificing comfort. These rooms typically come equipped with all of the same amenities as an air-conditioned room, such as a comfortable bed, television, and private bathroom.

One of the main benefits of a non-AC room is that it can be more affordable than an air-conditioned room. This can be a great option for budget-conscious travelers who are looking to save money on their stay. In addition, non-AC rooms can be a great option for those who prefer a more natural and cooler environment.

Despite the lack of air conditioning, non-AC rooms are still kept at a comfortable temperature, since most of the hotels are located in regions with moderate temperature. Guests can also open the windows for fresh air and natural light.
