About Us

A Luxuries Hotel with Nature

Situated in the land of peace, the birth place of Lord Gautam Buddha, offering great value for money with superb rooms and wonderful dining experience. Come and stay with us and allow us to make you feel at home with accommodate price

Located just aside of Lumbini Heritage site, Hotel UniversityInn is conveniently located 18 km away from the Gautam Buddha International Airport and only 20 km from the Bhairahawa City Market place. Since many of the distinguished NGO’s and INGO’s in Lumbini are in the vicinity and the Lumbini development trust office is only a few minutes walking distance. We provide a perfect place for our valued guests to rest, work or socialize
