About Us

Welcome to the Hotel University Inn, the premier destination for luxurious accommodations and unparalleled service. Our hotel is located in the heart of Lumbini, offering easy access to the city's top attractions, shopping, and dining.

Hotel UniversityInn is conveniently located 18 km away from the Gautam Buddha International Airport and only 20 km from the Bhairahawa City Market place. Since many of the distinguished NGO’s and INGO’s in Lumbini are in the vicinity and the Lumbini development trust office is only a few minutes walking distance. We provide a perfect place for our valued guests to rest, work or socialize.

Our 17 well-appointed rooms offer every comfort the traveler may need and we have a full range of facility for our distinguished guests. You can enjoy a full view of plain Terai farm land and UNESCO listed Monastic Zone of course. We offer the finest cuisine in our Kitchen as well dining for good appetite.

The food served are hygienic and timely. Our chef use their best skills to serve a delight on your table. You can enjoy the variety of delicious recipes Maya Devi Temple, sacred lake listed in UNESCO World Heritage Site, is 10 minutes’ walk from the hotel.
